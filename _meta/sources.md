## Source-quality notes
- Prefer primary literature and major meta-analyses over secondary summaries.
- For any longevity-influencer or supplement claim, trace back to the actual human
  trial. If it's animal/mechanistic only, it's T3/T4 — tag accordingly.
- Note funding/conflicts where relevant (industry-funded nutrition trials, supplement
  makers citing their own studies).
