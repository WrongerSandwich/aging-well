# Ranked actions — personal overlay template

> **How to use:** copy this file to `personal/ranked-actions.personal.md` (gitignored — see
> `.gitignore`). For each row, score **Tractability (T)** against your own life, compute
> **Priority = EO × T**, then re-sort the table descending by Priority. Resolve the
> *(conditional)* rows against `personal/profile.md`. See `synthesis/personalize.md` for the
> method and `_meta/scoring-rubric.md` for the two-views rationale.
>
> `EO` (Evidence-only = Impact × Certainty × Reversibility) is pre-filled from the general
> ranking in `synthesis/ranked-actions.md` — don't change it; it's the universal part.

**Tractability:** 3 = already do / near-zero friction · 2 = doable with effort · 1 = won't sustain.

## The actionable takeaway (write this last)
After you fill and sort the table, summarize the **gaps that matter** here — the
high-EO actions you are *not* yet doing. The top of a Priority list is usually dominated
by high-evidence things you *already* do (they need no new effort); your real to-do list
is the high-EO / lower-T items. `<2–4 sentences once the table is filled.>`
