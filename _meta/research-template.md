### Field definitions (reference)

**Tier** — T1: large human cohorts/RCTs/meta-analyses, hard endpoints · T2: smaller
RCTs / consistent observational / surrogate endpoints · T3: mechanistic/animal/small
or conflicted human · T4: speculative / hype-adjacent.

**Effect size** — quantify (HR, % change, absolute delta). Always name the comparator.

**Reversibility** — IRREVERSIBLE: doesn't recover (neurons, bone past peak, enamel,
cochlear/retinal cells, photodamage) · SLOW: recovers over months–years (fitness,
muscle, metabolic markers) · FAST: recovers quickly (acute sleep debt, hydration).

**Confidence** — your overall confidence in the claim AS APPLIED, after weighing tier,
consistency, and population fit. Distinct from tier.
